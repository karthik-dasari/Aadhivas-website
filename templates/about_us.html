We are as our name suggests Aadivas - which translates to 'original inhabitants'.

āadi meaning original (from the beginning) and vās meaning to dwell.
This term is now a collective term for the indegenious people of the Indian Subcontinent. Even in the era of rapidly-changing advanced technology we are deeply connected to our culture. This connection has a close interlink and can be seen in our unparalleled agriculture produce quality and foods. This comes from the vast experience and knowledge in agriculture methodologies & culinary talent which has accrued over time and passed on from one generation to another.
The origin of Aadivasi culture dates way back towards the end of Indus Valley Civilisation of Ancient India (1300 BCE). Harboring the various degrees of ancestral expertise, this rich culture now stretches from eastern Gujarat & Rajasthan in the west to the northeastern states of Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Mizoram and Nagaland.
We at Aadivas Foods wish to tell our story to the world as an agro exporter with a vision to provide the best value to our global customers in terms of fresh fruits, vegetables and other food products with top of the line quality standards.
Established in 2021, Aadivas Foods Pvt Ltd is registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India. Headquartered in Gumla (Jharkhand) the company operates with licenses from Indian Govt. including Importer-Exporter Code (IEC) license from DGFT-Ministry of Commerce and Industry, FSSAI (Food Safety & Standards Authority of India) license and a registered member of APEDA (Agriculture and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority).
